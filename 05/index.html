Week 5: 3D Printing

For this week, we had to 3D print something related to our projects as well as make a 3D scan. I printed out a minature model of the cat bowl that I was going to use for my final project, and I was fairly happy with the result! To figure out how to model it in, I followed this tutorial. After watching the tutorial, I sketched out a rough guide for the bowl in procreate.

After I made the sketch, I exported it to Fusion 360, then roughly sketched over it as well as moved the Fusion sketch.

I made the sketch 3D by rotating it around the axis, and then I had the file that I could move to PrusaSlicer to then take to the printer.

I exported the G-code from PrusaSlicer, then printed it out!
